about myself

Hi! My name is Jenny Na. I am a full-stack junior web developer with a background in healthcare. I was born and raised in South Korea, and later emigrated to the United States where I graduated from the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign with a Bachelor's degree in Kinesiology. I am able to adapt quickly to new environments, challenge myself, communicate effectively, and collaborate with teammates.

I'm passionate about exploring the growing intersection between technology and healthcare. I hope to make a tangible impact on both fields by continuously expanding and refining my skill set. I recently graduated from the University of Pennsylvania LPS Coding Bootcamp where I gained experience with HTML, CSS, Javascript, and JQuery. All of this has helped me build a project called Bubbles of which I am very proud.

I’m excited to use my new tech skills along with my strengths of working together with others to create something unique as well as to contribute to a development team.
